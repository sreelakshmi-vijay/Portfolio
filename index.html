About

I am a final-year Integrated M.Sc. Data Science student at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, focused on building machine learning systems that are robust to real-world noise, domain shift, and distributional variability.

My research explores how models generalize under challenging conditions — from cross-lingual sign language translation and geographically diverse wildfire risk prediction, to intrusion detection across unseen network environments and pharmacokinetic modeling with neural architectures.

I am particularly interested in the intersection of deep learning and applied sciences, with a long-term vision toward data-driven tools for climate risk, sustainability, and environmental systems.

Research Interests
Natural Language Processing (NLP) for socially impactful and multilingual AI systems
Robust and generalizable machine learning under domain shift, distributional variability, and limited-data settings
Interdisciplinary machine learning at the intersection of NLP, healthcare, and intelligent systems
